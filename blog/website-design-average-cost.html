Factors Influencing Website Design Costs

The cost of website design depends on various factors such as the size of the website, its functionality, platform selection, and customization needs. For instance, a basic website may cost around $1,000–$3,000, while a feature-rich e-commerce platform could range from $5,000 to $20,000. Additional expenses like responsive design, SEO optimization, and security features can add to the total cost. Understanding these elements helps businesses prioritize features based on their budgets and goals.
