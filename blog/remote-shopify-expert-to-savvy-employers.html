Does Shopify Remit Sales Tax?

No, Shopify does not automatically remit sales tax on behalf of merchants. However, Shopify provides tools to calculate, collect, and report sales tax. Merchants are responsible for remitting the collected taxes to the appropriate tax authorities. In some cases, third-party apps or integrations can help manage tax remittance efficiently.

Does Shopify Remit Taxes?

Shopify itself does not remit taxes for merchants.
