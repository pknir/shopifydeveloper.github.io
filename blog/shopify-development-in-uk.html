What Percentage Does Shopify Take in the UK?

Shopify charges transaction fees if you use a payment gateway other than Shopify Payments. In the UK, these fees range from 0.5% to 2% per transaction, depending on your Shopify plan. For example: Basic Plan: 2%
Shopify Plan: 1%
Advanced Plan: 0.5%
If you use Shopify Payments, there are no additional transaction fees, but standard credit card processing fees apply (usually around 1.9% + 20p for domestic cards). These fees can vary based on your plan and card type, making Shopify Payments a cost-effective option.
