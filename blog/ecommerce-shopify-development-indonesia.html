What Countries Are Not Supported by Shopify?

Shopify supports over 175 countries worldwide, but a few countries face restrictions due to legal or trade limitations. These typically include nations under international sanctions, such as North Korea, Iran, Syria, Cuba, and parts of Crimea. These restrictions arise due to compliance with global trade laws and payment gateway limitations. Additionally, some countries may lack local payment processor support, which could make Shopify less practical for merchants there. Entrepreneurs in unsupported regions often explore alternative e-commerce solutions or proxies to navigate these barriers. Shopify's documentation regularly updates its supported and restricted regions.
