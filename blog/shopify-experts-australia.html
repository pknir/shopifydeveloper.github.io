Are All Shopify Stores Dropshipping?

No, not all Shopify stores are dropshipping stores. While dropshipping is a popular business model on Shopify, it is just one of the many ways to operate an e-commerce store. In dropshipping, the store owner doesn't keep inventory in stock but instead partners with a supplier who fulfills orders directly to customers.
