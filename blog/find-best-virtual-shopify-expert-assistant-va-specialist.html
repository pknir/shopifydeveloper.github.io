Who pays for Shopify?

The store owner pays for Shopify. Shopify charges a monthly subscription fee for using its platform. Plans range from Basic Shopify ($29/month) to Advanced Shopify ($399/month), depending on your business needs. Additional costs may include transaction fees, app subscriptions, and custom themes. If you hire an expert, their fees are separate and negotiated directly with them.

Can I sell directly on Shopify?

Yes, Shopify allows you to sell directly through your online store. Once set up, you can list products, accept payments, and fulfill orders seamlessly. Shopify also supports integration with other sales channels, such as Facebook, Instagram, and Amazon, allowing you to reach a broader audience. Whether you’re selling physical products, digital goods, or services, Shopify offers all the tools to manage your business efficiently.
