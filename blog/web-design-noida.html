What are the golden rules of web designing?

The golden rules of web designing include:

Simplicity: Avoid clutter and keep the design clean and user-friendly.
Consistency: Maintain uniformity in fonts, colors, and layouts across all pages.
Responsiveness: Ensure the website is accessible on all devices and screen sizes.
Navigation: Provide clear and intuitive navigation for a seamless user experience.
Content Prioritization: Focus on valuable content with appropriate hierarchy and readability.
