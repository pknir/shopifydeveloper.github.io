Oberlo was officially discontinued in 2022. However, alternatives like Spocket, DSers, and Dropified are available in India and can be used with Shopify to manage dropshipping businesses.

No, Meesho is not a traditional dropshipping platform. It is a reseller app where individuals can sell products from suppliers to customers using social media, but it does not directly handle dropshipping logistics.
