Do I need a license to sell on Shopify in the USA?

No, Shopify itself does not require a specific license to sell. However, depending on your business and products:

Business License: Some states or cities may require a general business license.
Sales Tax Permit: If you sell taxable goods, you may need a sales tax permit for the states where you operate.
Special Licenses: Certain products, like alcohol or firearms, may require additional licensing.

Always check local regulations and consult a legal expert for compliance.
