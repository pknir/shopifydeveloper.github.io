What Cannot be outsourced in India?

Tasks requiring localized expertise, in-depth cultural knowledge, or physical presence cannot be easily outsourced. For example, on-site maintenance, certain legal services, and roles requiring immediate in-person interaction, such as facility management, often remain within the country of operation. Strategic decision-making and proprietary functions are also typically retained in-house.

Which jobs can't be outsourced in India?

Jobs that require direct physical presence, such as healthcare workers (e.g., nurses and doctors providing patient care), construction roles, or emergency services, cannot be outsourced. Additionally, positions demanding local cultural insight, such as marketing tailored to regional audiences, or roles involving high-security clearance, are less likely to be outsourced to India.
