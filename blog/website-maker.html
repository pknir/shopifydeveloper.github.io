What Websites Are Blocked in UAE?

The UAE blocks websites that conflict with its cultural, religious, or legal guidelines. Commonly restricted categories include adult content, gambling sites, and politically sensitive material. Specific examples include Skype (in the past), certain VPN services, and websites promoting illegal activities or extremist ideologies. Social media and VoIP services like WhatsApp calls may face restrictions, though alternatives like BOTIM are approved. The Telecommunications and Digital Government Regulatory Authority (TDRA) oversees internet filtering, ensuring compliance with UAE laws. Residents often use legal alternatives or licensed apps for communication and streaming while adhering to local regulations.
