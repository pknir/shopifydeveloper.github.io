What is a Shopify Partner?

A Shopify Partner is an individual or business that collaborates with Shopify to create custom solutions, including themes, apps, or store setups, for Shopify merchants. They help entrepreneurs build, grow, or enhance their e-commerce stores and earn revenue through referrals, app sales, or services offered. Shopify provides a partner dashboard to track earnings, clients, and resources.

How much do Shopify Partners get paid?

Shopify Partners earn income through various programs:
Referrals: Partners earn a percentage of the merchant’s subscription fee for every new Shopify store referred. This can be 20% of monthly fees for active subscriptions.
Services: Custom services like store setup or app development have no fixed limit; earnings depend on expertise and project scale.
App/Theme Sales: Developers selling apps or themes via the Shopify App Store or Theme Store earn a share of sales revenue, typically 85%.

What is the difference between a Shopify Partner and a Shopify Expert?

Shopify Partner: Anyone registered with Shopify’s Partner Program. They can refer merchants, create themes/apps, or offer services.
Shopify Expert: A more exclusive group within the Shopify Partner ecosystem, recognized for exceptional performance and expertise. Experts are listed in the Shopify Experts Marketplace for clients to hire.

Being a Shopify Expert often requires a proven track record and significant experience, whereas becoming a Shopify Partner has fewer entry barriers.
