Shopify works by offering an easy-to-use platform to build and manage an online store. Here's a basic step-by-step process:

What is Upwork Service Fee?

Upwork charges a service fee based on the total billings with a specific client. The standard fee is:
20% for the first $500 billed with a client.
10% for billings between $500.01 and $10,000.
5% for billings over $10,000. This fee applies to the total amount paid by the client and is deducted from the freelancer's earnings. The fee structure is designed to reward long-term, high-value relationships between clients and freelancers.
