Can 2 people own a Shopify store?

Yes, two or more people can own and manage a Shopify store collaboratively. Shopify allows you to add multiple staff accounts with different access levels to the store’s admin panel. This feature enables co-owners or team members to handle specific tasks like product management, order fulfillment, or marketing. If you’re in a partnership, clearly define roles and responsibilities to streamline operations. Keep in mind that Shopify’s pricing plans limit the number of staff accounts, so choose a plan that meets your needs.

What is the minimum budget for Shopify?

The minimum budget for starting a Shopify store includes the Basic Shopify plan, which costs $39 per month. Additional costs might include a domain name (around $10-$20 per year) and basic theme customization. If you’re managing the setup yourself, this budget might suffice. However, for professional setup and advanced customizations, you’ll need a higher budget, typically ranging from $500 to $2,000 or more, depending on your requirements. Factor in marketing and app subscriptions for a more comprehensive estimate.
